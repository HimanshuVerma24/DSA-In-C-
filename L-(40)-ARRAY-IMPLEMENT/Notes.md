(2) Enqueue Operation :- To Insert Elemnt In Queue From Rear.

    (ii) Move The Rear Index.

(3) Dequeue Operation:- To Delete Element From The Front. 
    (i) Check Queue IsEmpty. 
    (ii) Increase The Front.
